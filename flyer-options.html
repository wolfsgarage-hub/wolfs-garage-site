Event flyers — pick the fallback

Most scraped events do not have a flyer anywhere to fetch. Their source pages are shared calendar listings, so the only image available is the website's own logo, banner, or a sponsor's advert. Attaching those would be inventing a flyer, so we don't.

Example: four of your events all point at woodburndragstrip.com, whose page image is WoodburnDragStrip-banner-fbOG.jpg — the site's banner. Another four point at a calendar page whose image is an advert for StreetRod Warehouse. Scraping those would put a banner or someone else's ad on your events and call it their flyer.
Where real flyers DO come from
  • Community submissions — the new upload box on the submit form. Real flyer every time.
  • Your 5 carried-over events — Majestics, Pistons & Pints, C10s on the Capital, Windhaven, Grant County. Real flyers already in hand.
  • Occasional per-event pages — a genuine event poster, fetched only when the page is that event's own page, never a shared listing.

So how should a no-flyer event look?

Same real events from your queue in both. Pick A or B.

Option B — text only

No image box at all when there's no flyer. The words get the full width, so the listing reads like a calendar. Real flyers stand out hard because they're the only pictures on the page.
Trade-off: the grid is uneven — flyer cards are much taller than text cards.
